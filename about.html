About Us

About

At the Web Development Group, we have assembled a talented team of digital strategists, designers, developers, marketers, communications specialists, project managers and business developers to work in a collaborative manner on our client projects. We build on each other's expertise, working together in teams to generate great outcomes for projects.

What makes our company great? That’s simple. It’s the people and the culture. The team members at the Web Development Group are some of the most creative, interesting and dedicated people you'll ever meet, exactly the kind of people you'd love to work with.

History

In 2003, The Web Development Group made it's mission to create websites and digital solutions that would help grow businesses online. From dayone, the Web Development Group has been dedicated to bringing together the most creative digital strategists, designers, developers, marketers, communications specialists, project managers and business developers in the industry to deliver results-driven work for our clients.

Over 10 years later, our core mission of being united, creative, curious, and committed is stronger than ever.
